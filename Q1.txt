SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is a set of practices aimed at improving the visibility and ranking of a website or web page in search engine results pages (SERPs). 
The ultimate goal of SEO is to attract organic (non-paid) traffic to a website by optimizing various elements to align with search engine algorithms.

Here are some key aspects of SEO and why it's important:
1. visibility and ranking
2. Increased Traffic
3. Credibility and Trust 
4. Better User Experience 
5.Cost-Effectiveness
